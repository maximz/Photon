The Other Woman is an upcoming American comedy film directed by Nick Cassavetes and written by Melissa Stack. The film stars Cameron Diaz, Leslie Mann, Kate Upton, Nikolaj Coster-Waldau, Nicki Minaj, Lisa Maffia and Taylor Kinney.

The film will be distributed internationally by 20th Century Fox,[1] a subsidiary of the newly formed 21st Century Fox, after the disbandment of News Corporation.

Plot[edit]

After discovering her boyfriend (Nikolaj Coster-Waldau) is married, a woman (Cameron Diaz) tries to get her ruined life back on track. But when she accidentally meets the wife he’s been cheating on (Leslie Mann), she realizes they have much in common, and her sworn enemy becomes her greatest friend. When yet another affair is discovered (Kate Upton), all three women team up to plot mutual revenge on the cheating, lying, three-timing Mark.[2]

Production[edit]

The filming of The Other Woman began on April 29, 2013. Filming completed and the editing of sound, music and effects began as of August 27, 2013.[5] The film is filming in Downtown Manhattan, New York City.[6] The film will be produced by LBI Productions and will be distributed by 20th Century Fox.[1]

In December 2013, the first trailer for the film was released.[7][8]
