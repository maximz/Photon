Teen Wolf is an American supernatural drama developed by Jeff Davis loosely based upon the 1985 film of the same name, which premiered on June 5, 2011 on MTV. The series stars Tyler Posey as Scott McCall, a kind teenager who transforms into a werewolf after being bitten by one.

As of March 24, 2014[update], 48 episodes of Teen Wolf have aired, concluding the third season. A 6-episode web series, "Search for a Cure", produced and presented by AT&T can also be watched on the AT&T Facebook page, as well as MTV.com.

Season 4 (2014)[edit]

A fourth season of 12 episodes has been confirmed and will premiere on June 23, 2014 on MTV.[58]

Crystal Reed and Daniel Sharman won't be returning, being the first season of the show not to star Reed and the first since season two not to star Sharman.[59] New cast additions include Meagan Tandy as Braeden (who only appeared in 2 episodes of season 3),[60] Dylan Sprayberry as "Liam",[61] Khylin Rambo as Mason “an out-and-proud athlete” and Mason Dye as his best friend Garrett.[62] Dylan O'Brien and Jeff Davis confirmed in interviews that Malia Tate (Shelley Hennig) and Kira Yukimura (Arden Cho) would be regulars next season and join the pack.[63] Jill Wagner (Kate Argent) confirmed on Wolf Watch that she will be appearing more this season after her character made a surprise appearance in the season 3 finale. The first episode of Season 4 is titled "The Dark Moon".[64]
