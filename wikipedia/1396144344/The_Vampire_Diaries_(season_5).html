The Vampire Diaries, a one-hour American supernatural drama, was renewed for a fifth season by The CW on February 11, 2013.[3] The fifth season premiered on October 3, 2013, followed by the series premiere of the spin-off series The Originals.[4]

Production[edit]

Development[edit]

The series was renewed for a fifth season on February 11, 2013. The first writers meeting held on April 15, 2013.[30] Filming began on July 10, 2013.[31]

Cast[edit]

The cast features Nina Dobrev as Elena Gilbert, Katherine Pierce and Amara; Paul Wesley as Stefan Salvatore and Silas; Ian Somerhalder as Damon Salvatore; Steven R. McQueen as Jeremy Gilbert; Candice Accola as Caroline Forbes; Zach Roerig as Matt Donovan; Kat Graham as Bonnie Bennett and Michael Trevino as Tyler Lockwood, who is still under contract.

Within the first three episodes, four new characters were introduced: Nadia, a girl with a mission of revenge who has traveled around the world, Jesse, an upper-class boy who goes to college, Actors Olga Fonda and Kendrick Sampson were cast in the roles of Nadia and Jesse, respectively. On August 2, 2013, it was announced that Janina Gavankar will play Tessa.[32]
