The fifth season of Pretty Little Liars, based on the books of the same name by Sara Shepard, was renewed on March 26, 2013, and will premiere on June 10, 2014 on ABC Family.[2] Like the second season, the season will consist of 25 episodes, including a special holiday-themed episode, instead of a halloween episode.[3][4] It will also contain the 100th episode and will be written by showrunner I. Marlene King.[5] King also said on Twitter that they will do something special for the episode.[6] Shooting for the season began on March 24, 2014[7]

Overview[edit]

The fifth season will deal with the girls, and how they have to adjust to the new world where Alison is in.[2] In an interview with I. Marlene King, it was revealed that Ian Harding was asked back to the fifth season, meaning his character Ezra will be back. King also told about the clues Ezra has gathered saying: ‚ÄúIn all of his surveilling, Ezra‚Äôs found out some information that will help the girls truly unmask ‚ÄòA‚Äô ‚Äî so some good will come out of all this pain.‚Äù[8]

Casting[edit]

On March 12, 2014 it was announced that Tyler Blackburn will be returning as a series regular on the show after leaving halfway through the fourth season for the sister show; Ravenswood, and will return in the 100th episode.[9][10] It was reported that in the season premiere, a beloved character will be in great danger because of the casting of a paramedic and two surgeons for the first episode.[11]

  • Sasha Pieterse as Alison DiLaurentis

Recurring[edit]

  • Torrey DeVitto as Melissa Hastings
  • Vanessa Ray as CeCe Drake
  • Keegan Allen as Toby Cavanaugh
  • Brendan Robinson as Lucas Gottesman [13]
