Kanika Kapoor is an Indian Punjabi Sufi folk singer from the United Kingdom.[2] [3]

Career[edit]

In 2012, Kanika released her first music video. The music was by Tushar Chandra & Naveen Chouhan and the song featured the rapper. “Baby Doll” in Ragini MMS 2 (featuring Sunny Leone) is her latest song in India.

Discography[edit]

  • 2012: "Jugni Ji" (featuring Shortie), produced by Dr. Zeus

Filmography[edit]
