Jessie is a Disney Channel original series that revolves around Jessie Prescott (Debby Ryan), a young woman from Texas who travels to New York City and suddenly finds herself becoming a nanny to four unique children: Emma (Peyton List), Ravi (Karan Brar), Luke (Cameron Boyce) and Zuri Ross (Skai Jackson). The series first premiered on Disney Channel on September 30, 2011.[1] The second season premiered on October 5, 2012. In March 2013, the series was renewed for a third season, which began production in July 2013 and premiered on October 5, 2013.[2]

As of March 7, 2014[update], 62 episodes have aired over 3 seasons.
