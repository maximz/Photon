Main article: 2014 Sony Open Tennis

Andy Murray was the defending champion,[1] but lost in the quarterfinals to Novak Djokovic. This was the first tour event of any level in the Open Era where two semifinalists issued walkovers to their opponents, resulting in no matches in the semifinal round.[2]
