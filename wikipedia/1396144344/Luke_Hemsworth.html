Luke Hemsworth (born 1981) is an Australian actor who is best known for his role as Nathan Tyson in the TV series Neighbours.

Early life[edit]

Born in Melbourne, Australia, to Leonie, an English teacher, and Craig Hemsworth, a social-services counselor,[1] he is the eldest of three boys. His brothers are actors Chris Hemsworth and Liam Hemsworth.[2]

Career[edit]

Hemsworth started his career on the Australian soap opera Neighbours as Nathan Tyson. Mainly a television actor, Hemsworth has appeared in TV series such as The Saddle Club, Blue Heelers, Last Man Standing, All Saints, and Satisfaction. In 2012, he starred in the 6-part miniseries Bikie Wars: Brothers in Arms as Gregory "Shadow" Campbell.

Personal life[edit]

Hemsworth has been married to wife Samantha since 2007 and they have four children together, daughters Holly (b. 2009), Ella (b. 2010), and Harper Rose (b. 2012) and son Alexandre (b. 2013) [3]
