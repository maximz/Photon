Sam & Cat is an American sitcom that premiered on Nickelodeon on June 8, 2013.[1] It is a crossover/spin-off and sequel of both iCarly and Victorious, starring Jennette McCurdy and Ariana Grande, Sam Puckett and Cat Valentine respectively. The series is based on both these girls as they buddy up as roommates and start a babysitting business to fund their adventures. The second half of Season 1 began to air its episodes starting in January 2014, which has been met with higher ratings, in comparison to the first half of the season.

1 "#Pilot" Steve Hoefer Dan Schneider June 8, 2013 (2013-06-08) 101 4.2[6][7]

Set after the events of "iGoodbye", Sam Puckett (Jennette McCurdy, reprising her role from iCarly), arrives at a food truck in Los Angeles, and sees a red-headed girl get dumped into a garbage truck. Sam, being the only witness, runs after the truck to save her. Later, Sam wakes the girl after fainting in the truck. The girl introduces herself as Cat Valentine (Ariana Grande, reprising her role from Victorious). To thank Sam, Cat lets her bathe and rest at her grandmother Nona's apartment. The next day, after coming from school, Cat learns Sam took her Nona to a retirement community called Elderly Acres. While leaving the apartment, they run into three kids, Chloe, Max, and Darby, that Cat's Nona was suppose to babysit. They decide to take them with them to Elderly Acres. The three kids drive an electric wheelchair to Inside-Out Burger. Cat talks to her Nona and she lets her stay in Elderly Acres. Sam and Cat find the three kids at Inside-Out Burger, thanks to Dice (Cameron Ocasio), a kid who lives in the building. Sam and Cat save a manager after he faints of exhaustion from chasing the three kids on the wheelchair. They are rewarded with free cheeseburgers for the rest of their lives. Afterward, Sam gets ready to leave; Cat, not wanting to stay alone, wants her to stay, and thus they become roommates. Sam and Cat celebrate with free cheeseburgers at Inside-Out Burger.

Guest stars: Griffin Kane as Max, Emily Skinner as Chloe.

Guest Starring: Noah Munck as Gibby Gibson, Reed Alexander as Nevel Papperman, and Danielle Morrow as Nora. Characters from iCarly.

Note: The premise of this episode is very similar to The Silence of the Lambs. Jerry Trainor makes an appearance as Crazy Steve from Drake and Josh. He is seen wearing the signature Premiere Movie Theater vest.
