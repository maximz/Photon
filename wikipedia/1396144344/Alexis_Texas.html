Alexis Texas (born May 25, 1985)[3] is an American pornographic actress. She began her career in pornography in 2006 at the age of 21.

Biography[edit]

Alexis Texas was born in the Panama military base but was raised in the Castroville area of Texas.[4] Texas is of German, Puerto Rican and Norwegian descent.[4][5] Her first scene was with Jack Venice in Shane's World's College Amateur Tour In Texas, filmed in October 2006, followed by a couple of scenes for Bang Bros in Florida. She then moved to Los Angeles and began shooting scenes for the LA Direct modeling agency in March 2007.[6] Released in February 2008, Discovering Alexis Texas was directed by the adult actress Belladonna.[7] In 2009 she formed her own business, Alexis Texas Entertainment, which is a subsidiary of Starlet Entertainment Group. Through SEG, she launched her official membership-based website.[8] She featured on the cover of Genesis magazine in April 2009,[9] and Hustler magazine's 35th anniversary edition in June 2009.[10] In 2010, she was named by Maxim as one of the 12 top female stars in porn.[11] In 2011, she made her crossover debut to mainstream movies starring in the comedy horror film Bloodlust Zombies.[12][13] In 2012, she appeared in the music video for Bandz A Make Her Dance by Juicy J.[14] The same year, she signed an exclusive one-year contract with the company Adam & Eve.[15] In 2013 she was one of the 16 actresses portrayed in the Deborah Anderson's documentary film Aroused.[16]

Personal life[edit]

Alexis was married to pornographic actor, Mr. Pete.[17]
