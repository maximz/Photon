Sasha Pieterse (born February 17, 1996)[1] is an American actress and singer. She is known for her role as Alison DiLaurentis on ABC Family's series Pretty Little Liars.

Early life[edit]

Sasha Pieterse was born in Johannesburg, South Africa on February 17, 1996. Immigrating to the United States when she was three years old, she was raised in Las Vegas before moving to Los Angeles.[3] She became accustomed to an career in entertainment at an early age, as her parents were a professional acrobatic dance team who performed all over the world.[3] She soon met with an agent who quickly signed her to an acting and modeling contract. She started in the business at the age of 4 1/2 working on commercials and print work.[4]

At 6 years old, Sasha Pieterse got her start in television, co-starring in 2002 as Buffy on the Warner Bros. comedy series Family Affair. In 2004, she appeared in Stargate SG-1. She started to rise to fame as an actress when she played in House portraying a young girl named Andie with terminal cancer. Her performance in House M.D was later considered for a daytime Emmy in 2005.[4] In 2005, she appeared in her first film The Adventures of Shark Boy and Lava Girl cast as Marissa the "Ice Princess" who is the daughter of Mr. Electricidad.[4] Pieterse has done numerous television commercials including long-running national spots for “Nesquick” and “Firestone Tires”. She was featured on a billboard for “Supercuts” that appeared throughout the country then she sang and modeled in the Macy’s Passport Fashion Show, performing throughout California.[4] During 2006 she filmed for a feature starring Sarah Michelle Gellar, The Air I Breathe and she landed a lead in a Hallmark Hall of Fame Movie as Maggie in Claire, both were released in 2007.[4]

Career[edit]

Acting[edit]

In 2006, Pieterse stars as Good Luck Chuck as a young goth girl named Anisha whom while playing a game seven minutes in heaven at a party in 1985, 12-year-old Charles "Chuck" Logan (Connor Price) refuses to have sex with Anisha. In retaliation, Anisha places a curse on Chuck, so that every single woman he sleeps with will break up with him and marry the next man who asks her out.[4] Also in a short-lived series called Wanted with her former Family Affair co-star, Gary Cole as Millie Rose for two episodes.[4]

Pieterse was cast on Pretty Little Liars as Alison DiLaurentis, the former "queen bee" of her clique and the most popular and beautiful girl in her school before her disappearance and death.[4] Towards the end of the third season, it is revealed that she may not be dead after all and is "Red Coat" - the lead tormentor towards the Liars. Being only 18, she is the youngest actress on the show, all her co-stars being in their early to late 20s. She also became the character of Vivian Darkbloom who was Alison's secret identity that she used to hide from A and spy on A. Alison wore a long red trench coat and a black wig to become Vivian.[4] Pretty Little Liars' producer I. Marlene King announced that Pieterse's character Ali would be playing a more prominent role in season 4.[5] On March 26, 2013, ABC Family picked up the show for a fifth season.[6][7]

In 2009, Pieterse made an appearance in Without a Trace as Daphne Stevens in the episode "Wanted" and Pieterse landed a recurring role as Amanda Strazzulla who is the daughter of Lydia who abandoned her because she was too young to raise her.[4] In 2011, she appeared as Amy Loubalu who is Josh (Matt Prokop) love interest in the Disney Channel Original Movie Geek Charming also as a teenage girl in the movie X-Men: First Class. On October 4, 2012, ABC Family renewed Pretty Little Liars for a fourth season.[8] Filming began on March 14, 2013.[9]

Music[edit]

Pieterse describes her music as "country with southern rock". Pieterse has posted on her Instagram the official artwork of her debut Country single, titled "This Country is Bad Ass".[10] Her debut single was released on April 12, 2013, Pieterse added about her debut single “I just love this country so much and I would never have been able to be where I am if I wasn’t in America so we decided to come up with this [track].”[11] Her second single "R.P.M" was released on June 13.[12]

Her third single "I Can't Fix You" was released on July 12, 2013.[13][14] On 2013 December 10, she released her fourth single "No", an upbeat track about standing strong in your convictions, even when your up-to-no-good, loser ex comes around and begs you for another chance.[15][16]

Personal life[edit]

Pieterse's personal interests include, cooking, baking, photography, traveling, Jewelry making which she often makes for her character Alison, Rock climbing and Interior & Exterior Design.[2] She also is a fan of the Green Bay Packers, according to her personal Instagram page.
