Witney Carson is an American dancer/actress/choreographer specializing in Latin ballroom dance. Originally from American Fork, Utah,[1] she was born October 17, 1993. Witney was only 18 at the time she was a contestant on So You Think You Can Dance. She continued her professional dancing career as a troupe dancer on Dancing with the Stars Season 16 and Season 17. Witney was promoted to being a professional dancer on Season 18. She is partnering Australian singer Cody Simpson.[2]

Throughout her dance career, Carson has worked with dancers and choreographers such as Mia Michaels, Nappytabs, Jean-Marc Généreux, Mark Ballas, Julianne Hough.

Early life[edit]

Carson was born in American Fork, Utah to Tyler Carson and Jill Johnson- Carson. She has two brothers and one sister. She started her career as a dancer at the age of 3, training in different dance styles including ballet, jazz dance, hip hop dance, ballroom dance as well as tap dance among many others. She has competed and performed all over the world and has won several award and scholarships throughout her career.

So You Think You Can Dance[edit]

At the age of 18, Carson auditioned for the 9th season of FOX 's hit show So You Think You Can Dance. She made it through to the top twenty finalists. Her partner was ballet dancer and season winner Chehon Wespi-Tschopp. When only ten dancers remained the contestants switched partners to a new all-star each week. As she proceeded to the top 6 dancers, Carson partnered with dancers Nick Lazzarini, Stephen "Twitch" Boss and Marko Germar before she was eliminated after the Week 7 round which aired on September 2012.[3]

She returned as an all-star on Season 10 partnering top 6 dancer Paul Karmiryan and season winner, Du-shaunt "Fik-shun" Stegall .[4]

Dancing with the Stars[edit]

Witney was announced as a troupe dancer on reality television series, Dancing with the Stars in March 2013.[5] Carson, along with DWTS pro dancer, Lindsay Arnold, were both contestants on SYTYCD Season 9.[5] She continued dancing and choreographing for the troupe on season 17.

On season 18, Carson was promoted to professional dancer partnering with singer Cody Simpson.[2]

East Side Story[edit]

Carson is part of the dancing cast of the 2013 movie East Side Story in the role of "Jennifer". She joins former SYTYCD winners Russell Ferguson (season 6), and Chehon Wespi-Tschopp (Season 9).[6]
