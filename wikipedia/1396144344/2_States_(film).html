2 States is an upcoming Indian romantic comedy film directed by Abhishek Varman based on the 2009 novel of the same name written by Chetan Bhagat. It is produced by Sajid Nadiadwala and Karan Johar.[1] The film stars Arjun Kapoor and Alia Bhatt in lead roles.[2][3] The film is slated to release on 18 April 2014.[4]

Plot[edit]

2 States is a love story of a couple Krish Malhotra (Arjun Kapoor) and Ananya Swaminathan (Alia Bhatt). They meet at Indian Institute of Management Ahmedabad and during the course of time, fall in love with each other. Problems gradually begin creeping up after the couple take a decision to get married, because Krish and Ananya come from different states of the country, and have drastically different backgrounds. Krish, happens to be from Delhi and comes from a Punjabi household, whereas Ananya comes from a Tamil Brahmin household from Chennai. The couple decide to get married only upon the agreement of their respective parents. Chaos ensues when the couples’ parents meet each other. The clash of cultures puts forth a lot of obstacles, which Krish and Ananya must fight in order to end up together.

Production[edit]

Casting[edit]

First choice for the movie has been Shahrukh Khan and Asin Thottumkal and then been approached by Ranbir Kapoor and Saif Ali Khan. Finally settled with Arjun Kapoor for the lead. [7] Actors Alia Bhatt and Arjun Kapoor are paired opposite each other, Amrita Singh and Ronit Roy are casted as Arjun Kapoor's parents and Revathy and Shiv Kumar Subramaniam are casted as Alia Bhatt's parents. [8] In an Interview, Alia said that her character in the film was much different from that played by Deepika Padukone in Chennai Express.[9]

Filming[edit]

Principal photography commenced from 29 January 2013 with the shooting of a song.[10] Parts of the film were shot at Chennai, Delhi, IIM-A and Gol Limda Bhajiya House near Astodia Darwaza in Ahmedabad in August, 2013.[11][12] Alia would be sporting an Indian Fusion look in the film and her costumes have been designed by Manish Malhotra.[9]Speaking about her character, Alia said that she is a City Girl who speaks Tamil only with her parents.[13]
