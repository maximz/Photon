List of House of Cards episodes

Jump to: navigation, search

House of Cards is an American television drama series created by Beau Willimon for Netflix. The first season, based on the BBC miniseries of the same name and the series of books by Michael Dobbs,[1] premiered exclusively via Netflix's web streaming service on February 1, 2013.[2]

Kevin Spacey stars as Representative Francis Underwood, the Majority Whip, who, after being passed up for the position of Secretary of State, devises a revenge plot against President Garrett Walker (Michael Gill). The first season also stars Robin Wright and Kate Mara.
