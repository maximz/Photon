Avengers Assemble is an animated television series based on the comic book super hero team known as the Avengers which premiered on May 26, 2013, on Teletoon in Canada and Disney XD internationally.

Episodes[edit]

Season 1 (2013-2014)[edit]

No. in
series		 No. in
season		 Episode title Director Writer(s) U.S. original airdate Prod. No.
1 1 "The Avengers Protocol: Part 1" Eric Radomski Man of Action May 26, 2013 (2013-05-26) 101

After having been disbanded, the Avengers reunite when Red Skull (who is dying from the imperfection of his Super Soldier Serum) and MODOK kidnap Captain America. The heroes must come together to rescue Captain America before Red Skull can transfer his mind into Captain America's body.

Introducing characters: Black Widow, Captain America, Falcon, Hawkeye, Hulk, HYDRA, Iron Man, J.A.R.V.I.S., MODOK, Nick Fury, Red Skull, Thor

Introduction in cameo: Trapster, Wizard, Klaw 
2 2 "The Avengers Protocol: Part 2" Eric Radomski Man of Action May 26, 2013 (2013-05-26) 102

The Avengers retreat back to Avengers Mansion to save Tony Stark's life after his armor is stolen by Red Skull at the last minute of a successful battle. MODOK uses his technology to mind-control the Avengers causing them to brutally attack each other while Red Skull infiltrates the Mansion to carry out a devious plot that puts the entire city of Manhattan at risk.

Introducing characters: J. Jonah Jameson (voice only)

Introduction in cameo: Attuma, Doctor Doom, Dracula 
3 3 "Ghost of a Chance" Jeff Allen Man of Action & Paul Giacoppo July 7, 2013 (2013-07-07) 103

Falcon stands alone to save his team and stop a full-scale invasion of Earth after the Avengers fall victim to the body-swapping Space Phantoms.

Introducing characters:Doctor Doom, (in a cameo from a Hulk rampage clip), The Space Phantoms 
4 4 "The Serpent of Doom" Tim Eldred Man of Action July 14, 2013 (2013-07-14) 104

Thor believes he must sacrifice himself to save the world from Doctor Doom when he summons the Midgard Serpent using a powerful Asgardian weapon stolen from Ulik.

Introducing characters:Doctor Doom (official debut), Midgard Serpent, Ulik 
5 5 "Blood Feud" Jeff Allen Man of Action, Kevin Burke & Chris "Doc" Wyatt July 21, 2013 (2013-07-21) 105

The Avengers face-off with Dracula and his vampire army after Dracula partially converts Black Widow into a vampire. It's a race against time to stop Black Widow from becoming a full vampiress.

Introducing characters: :Dracula,(officially), Vampires 
6 6 "Super-Adaptoid" Tim Eldred Man of Action & Ed Valentine July 28, 2013 (2013-07-28) 106

The Avengers are out-gunned by the high-tech, power-mimicking Super-Adaptoid when it is created by Red Skull and Justin Hammer. Captain America is the last man standing against the ultimate artificial intelligence.

Introducing characters: Super-Adaptoid, Justin Hammer 
7 7 "Hyperion" Jeff Allen Man of Action & Jacob Semahn August 4, 2013 (2013-08-04)[1] 107

An all-powerful super-being named Hyperion arrives to save the Earth. But when his methods to keep peace on Earth become too extreme, the Avengers must stand up to him to save humanity from total domination.

Introducing characters: Hyperion, Wrecker 
8 8 "Molecule Kid" Tim Eldred Man of Action & Danielle Wolff August 11, 2013 (2013-08-11)[2] 108

Hawkeye and Black Widow take on a covert S.H.I.E.L.D. mission to bring in Molecule Man's son Aaron where Nick Fury wants the mission to be kept private from Iron Man. But when he's revealed to have a world-bending weapon that was wielded by Molecule Man, they realize keeping their mission a secret may be a fatal mistake when A.I.M. Agents, MODOK, and Super-Adaptoid target Molecule Man's son so that Red Skull can shape the world into his own vision.

Introducing characters: A.I.M., Molecule Man (flashback and mention only), Aaron Reece

Notes: During the flashback sequence, several of the Avengers are depicted using the character models from the previous series The Avengers: Earth's Mightiest Heroes. Also, there was the Tri-Carrier and a reference to Nick Fury's program for teenage heroes as seen in Ultimate Spider-Man 
9 9 "Depth Charge" Jeff Allen Man of Action & Jay Faerber September 15, 2013 (2013-09-15) 109

During a fight with a giant monster, Hulk figures out that something has caused it to run scared into Manhattan as the Avengers notice a tidal wave approaching. They discover that Attuma is trying to sink Manhattan. Hulk is forced to prove his strength or perish trying.

Introducing characters: Attuma (officially), Atlanteans 
10 10 "The Doomstroyer" Tim Eldred Man of Action & Paul Giacoppo September 22, 2013 (2013-09-22) 110

Doctor Doom takes command of the Destroyer armor. Thor has to convince the team to trust the one person who may be able to stop him....Loki!

Introducing characters: Loki, Destroyer 
11 11 "Hulked Out Heroes" Jeff Allen Man of Action & Jacob Semahn September 29, 2013 (2013-09-29) 111

The Avengers learn what it's like to walk a mile in Hulk's shoes when an unstable gamma energy virus by Red Skull and the Blood Brothers turns them into raging monsters. The unlikely duo of Hulk and Black Widow are the last line of defense between the city and the Hulk-Vengers before the virus causes them to detonate.

Introducing characters: Blood Brothers 
12 12 "Avengers: Impossible" Tim Eldred Man of Action & Jacob Semahn October 20, 2013 (2013-10-20) 112[3]

The Avengers' world is turned upside down when the shape-shifting mischief-maker Impossible Man makes Falcon the star of his own action movie following their fight with the Wrecking Crew. Things get worse when a Chitauri scouting party arrive where they have been pursuing Impossible Man.

Introducing characters: Chitauri, Impossible Man, Wendigo, Wrecking Crew (Thunderball, Bulldozer, and Piledriver) 
13 13 "In Deep" Jeff Allen Man of Action, Kevin Burke & Chris "Doc" Wyatt November 17, 2013 (2013-11-17) 113[4]

Iron Man and Captain America disguise themselves as Grim Reaper and Crossbones (whom the Avengers previously captured) to infiltrate Red Skull's Cabal, but when their disagreements over strategy cause their cover to get blown, they're forced to take on the entire Cabal alone.[5]

Introducing characters: Grim Reaper, Crossbones 
14 14 "Hulk's Day Out" Tim Eldred Man of Action & Jay Faerber November 24, 2013 (2013-11-24) 114[6]

When Hulk crash-lands on Earth with amnesia, The Avengers reconstruct a day-in-the-life of Hulk to find out what world-ending threat could have knocked out The Strongest Thing There Is.[7]

Introducing characters: Thing, Spider-Man, Glorian, Uatu the Watcher, Devil Dinosaur 
15 15 "Planet Doom" Jeff Allen Man of Action & Paul Giacoppo December 8, 2013 (2013-12-08) 115[8]

After a brief argument with his father Odin over his obsession of Midgard, Thor returns to find Earth under the rule of Doctor Doom who has changed the past drastically to ensure that the Avengers never formed. Thor must team up with this timeline's Defenders to defeat Doctor Doom and undo the time change that Doctor Doom committed.[9]

Introducing characters: Defenders (Slinger, Bullseye, Snap), Black Bride, Punisher, Bruce Banner, Brain Trust, Odin, Heimdall, Volstagg

Notes: Black Bride wears the same mask as Madame Masque. 
16 16 "Bring on the Bad Guys" Tim Eldred Man of Action & Eugene Son February 16, 2014 (2014-02-16) 116[10]
While meeting with Attuma, Dracula, and MODOK, Red Skull decides that the Cabal needs to be more cooperative if they are going to defeat the Avengers. The Avengers start to see how tough the Cabal is and how much strength they'll need to take it down. 
17 17 "Savages" Jeff Allen Man of Action & Danielle Wolff March 2, 2014 (2014-03-02)[11] 117
When Tony becomes too reliant on his tech, Captain America challenges him to go without it for one full day. The challenge is accepted but made far more complicated when Tony takes them to the Savage Land. However, they quickly regret the decision when they discover Justin Hammer in his latest "get into the Cabal scheme" that involves mining the Savage Land for a massive Vibranium deposit.[12] 
18 18 "Mojo World" Tim Eldred Man of Action & Jacob Semahn March 9, 2014 (2014-03-09)[11] 118

Hulk and Hawkeye suddenly find themselves stuck in a death match at the hands of the intergalactic broadcasting master, Mojo.[13]

Introducing characters: Mojo, Torgo, Warwolves

Introduction in cameo: Skrulls, Watchers, Star-Lord 
19 19 "The Ambassador" Jeff Allen Man of Action, Kevin Burke & Chris "Doc" Wyatt March 16, 2014 (2014-03-16)[11] 119
The Avengers put their lives at risk to protect none other than Doctor Doom (at the time when he is speaking at the United Nations) from the Cabal (due to Doctor Doom not taking Red Skull's offer to join them). But is that all that Doctor Doom needs?[14] 
20 20 "All-Father's Day" Tim Eldred Man of Action & Jacob Semahn March 23, 2014 (2014-03-23)[11] 120

Odin comes to the Avengers Tower demanding his son cease his "fun" on Earth and return to Asgard. Thor promises that if he can't prove the good he does on Earth he'll return home forever. Of course this proves to be a problem when Mangog arrives on Earth.[15]

Introducing characters: Mangog, Zzzax 
21 21 "By the Numbers" Jeff Allen TBA March 30, 2014 (2014-03-30)[11] 121
The Avengers and the Cabal have a huge confrontation that leaves the team forever changed. Tony also learns that "the stats" can't be trusted every time - there's no substitute for human intuition.[16] 
22 22 "Guardians and Space Knights" TBA April 6, 2014 (2014-04-06) 122
When Galactus attacks Earth, Iron Man is able to lead him to another planet but discovers that it is populated and under the protection of the Guardians of the Galaxy.[17] 
23 23 "One Little Thing" TBA April 13, 2014 (2014-04-13) 123
While Iron Man and Ant Man are trying to stabilize particles that can shrink or grow matter, Falcon gives them terrible news - his mom is coming to visit, and she has no idea he's an Avenger.[18]
