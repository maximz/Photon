Austin Mahone (born April 4, 1996) is an American pop singer-songwriter. He is currently signed to Young Money Entertainment, Cash Money Records and Republic Records.

Background

Mahone was born in San Antonio, Texas on April 4, 1996. His father died when he was a year and a half old, and he was raised by his single mother, Michelle.[3] He attended Lady Bird Johnson High School.[4] He first started posting videos on YouTube with his friend Alex Constancio in June 2010.[5] Music videos followed by in January 2011, and he began to build an online following.[6] In October 2011, Mahone posted a video covering Justin Bieber's "Mistletoe".[7][8]

On August 28, 2012, Mahone announced that he had been officially signed to Chase/Universal Republic Records.[9] Mahone's first single was "11:11". On June 5, 2012, Mahone released a second single called "Say Somethin". In 2013, he was named one of MTV's Artists To Watch.[10]

Mahone was chosen as one of the opening acts of Taylor Swift's Red Tour.[11] In November 2012, Mahone signed a deal to become the new "Teen Ambassador" for Lil Wayne's Trukfit fashion clothing line.[12] He has filmed commercials for McDonald's[13] and Hot Nuts (Mexican snack).[14] In December 2013, Mahone was named a "Digital and Brand Strategist" for Aquafina’s FlavorSplash, a new line of flavored sparkling water targeted towards teens.[15]
