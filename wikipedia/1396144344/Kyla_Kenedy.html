Kyla Kennedy White (born 4 February 2003), also known by her stage name Kyla Kenedy is an American actress. She is best known for her starring role on the TV movie Raising Izzie and for her recurring role on AMC's hit television series, The Walking Dead as Mika Samuels.[1]
