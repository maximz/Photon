List of Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles (2012 TV series) episodes

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to: navigation, search


Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles is an American computer animated series based on the eponymous franchise that premiered on September 29, 2012 on Nickelodeon in the United States[1] and on YTV in Canada.[2] It premiered on Eleven in Australia at 7:30am on Sunday, June 30, 2013.[3]
