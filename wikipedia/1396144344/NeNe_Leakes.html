Linnethia Monique "NeNe" Leakes /ˈniːniː/ (née Johnson; born December 13, 1967)[1][2][3] is an American television personality and actress. In October 2008, she came to prominence after being cast in the reality television series The Real Housewives of Atlanta, the third spin-off from The Real Housewives of Orange County.

Life and career[edit]

While working as a stripper, she met Gregg Leakes in 1996. Gregg Leakes is a real estate investor and business consultant. They married in 1997 and had son Brentt on February 22, 1999. Gregg has older children with his exes.

In 2008, Leakes made her first appearance on The Real Housewives of Atlanta.[4] After the first season, she co-wrote with Denene Milner the book Never Make The Same Mistake Twice. Leakes has been a part of the cast for all six seasons. Leakes played sassy secretary Rocky Roades in the NBC comedy The New Normal.[5][6] Ryan Murphy cast NeNe on his series Glee in the third season in a recurring role as synchronized swim coach and Olympic bronze-medalist Coach Roz Washington.[7][8][9][10]

In 2010, Nene and Gregg separated, and NeNe filed for divorce on April 29, 2010. The divorce was finalized September 29, 2011, after season four of The Real Housewives had finished shooting.[11] In October 2011, Leakes released a memoir describing her experiences with abuse called Never Make the Same Mistake Twice: Lessons on Love and Life Learned the Hard Way.[12] NeNe and Gregg Leakes reunited and announced that they were engaged in January 2013.[13][14] Bravo filmed their wedding planning and ceremony for a spin-off entitled I Dream of NeNe: The Wedding.[15] NeNe and Gregg remarried on June 22, 2013, at the InterContinental Buckhead Hotel in Atlanta.[16] In 2011, Leakes was a contestant on the eleventh installment of Donald Trump's NBC series Celebrity Apprentice.[17] During the tenth episode of Apprentice, after arguments with fellow cast member Star Jones, Leakes walked off the show.[18][19] Leakes failed to win any money for her selected charity, "My Sister's House".

Leakes has guest-hosted Anderson Live,[20] The Talk, and appeared in Betty White's Off Their Rockers[21] and The Price Is Right.[22]

Nene Leakes resides in Duluth, Georgia, a suburb of Atlanta.[23] She also rents a house in the Hollywood Hills West, Los Angeles.[24][25][26] NeNe has a son named Bryson "Brice" Rashard Bryant (born December 1989) from a previous relationship. Leakes has a granddaughter, Bri'Asia Bryant (born June 2012).[27] Leakes rising popularity has made her the center of multiple parodies. One example is Celebrity Smacktalker's Competition[28] while another example was on The Bonnie Hunt Show by Niecy Nash.[29]

On March 4th, 2014 she was announced to be one of the celebrities who will compete in the 18th season of Dancing with the Stars. She is paired with professional dancer, Tony Dovolani.[30]
