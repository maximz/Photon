Ye Hai Mohabbatein, sometimes spelled Yeh Hai Mohabbatein (English: This is Love), is an Indian soap opera that airs on Star Plus. It premiered on 3 December 2013[1] and airs Monday through Saturday nights. It is produced by Ekta Kapoor of Balaji Telefilms.[2][3] It is based on Manju Kapur's novel Custody.

Plot summary[edit]

Ye Hai Mohabbatein follows the story of Raman and Ishita who are connected by their common love for Raman's little daughter Ruhi. Ishita is a Tamilian[4] whereas Raman is a Punjabi.[5] Ishita, who is unable to bear children, shares a very close, motherly relationship with Ruhi, filling the void of a mother in Ruhi's life, while filling the void of a child in her own. She is friendly, warm and caring while Raman is bitter and hard from the days of his divorce.

These two polar opposites with different cultures and traditions clash when Raman and Ishita get married for Ruhi. In ways that are funny, heartbreaking and endearing all at the same time, Raman and Ishita's relationship evolves revolving around Ruhi, arguments and headbutting to becoming something more personal between the two of them, something more like love.
