The Americans is an American television drama series created by Joe Weisberg, which premiered on January 30, 2013 on the cable network FX. Set during the Cold War period in the 1980s, The Americans is the story of Elizabeth (Keri Russell) and Philip Jennings (Matthew Rhys), two Soviet KGB officers posing as American citizens and a married couple.[1]

As of March 26, 2014[update], 18 episodes of The Americans have aired.
