MOBIQUANT TECHNOLOGIES is a pionneer provider in enterprise-grade mobile security management[citation needed], covering the concepts of (MSM) Mobile Security Management and (m-UTM) Mobile Unified Threat Management. Its innovative[peacock term] mobile management platform allows unprecedented feature-rich capabilities for IT teams daily operations. It includes NXSafeWave VPN, NXSafevoice, NXDefender Suite, NXOffice a BYOD container, NXTrustPKI and many others unique mobile IT patented technologies. Those solutions conver the management of any device based on Apple iOS iPhones, iPad , Google Android, RIM Blackberry, Samsung Tizen, Knox, Microsoft Windows Phone smartphones and TabletPC.

Mobiquant Technologies was the first company in the world[citation needed] (Mobile Congress 2007) to build a multiplatform and MDM/MSM solution. As well as the first to address the IT and Security Community while new comers (2009 and so) were betting on carrier side. This makes our current success on the market based not on the marketing side of things but technical and innovation value addition[peacock term]. First DODs running a MSM/MDM solution have chosen Mobiquant technology.[citation needed]

The company now has a worldwide presence[citation needed], and has gained significant recognition among the IT and Security Community[according to whom?] for the mobile security innovation seen in its solutions and which allows it to continue to expand and develop.

Mobiquant has a range of innovative solutions[peacock term] for mobile security declined in a hardware or cloud version.

Mobiquant Technologies research teams address the challenges of mobile security management and granular control of user activity in mobile environments.[1] The company was founded by Reda Zitouni, a security industry professional and D.P. Shah, former marketing manager at HP mobile division.[2]

The company competes with similar services offered by IBM, Symantec, McAfee and Research in Motion.
