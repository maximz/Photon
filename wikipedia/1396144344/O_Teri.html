O Teri is an upcoming Hindi comedy film produced by Atul Agnihotri,[1] featuring Pulkit Samrat, Bilal Amrohi, Sarah-Jane Dias,[2]Sara Loren,[3] and Mandira Bedi. The film stars Pulkit Samrat and newcomer Bilal Amrohi as two journalists, who come across a big scam.[4] The film is slated for release on 28 March 2014. Umesh Bhisht directed. The music is composed by GJ Singh, who also scored music for title track of Punjabi Movie R.S.V.P.. The premise of the movie is heavily lifted from yesteryear classic, Jaane Bhi Do Yaaro

Location[edit]

The movie is shot primarily at the iconic Mindmill Corporate Towers in Noida, NCR. The building boasts of a Neo-Classical architecture adapted from the Parthenon in Athens, Greece.
