The sixth season of the American reality talent show The Voice began on February 24, 2014[1] on NBC. The show is hosted by Carson Daly. Adam Levine and Blake Shelton returned as coaches for their sixth season, while Shakira and Usher returned for their second season. Christina Aguilera took another hiatus from the show, while CeeLo Green departed the series at the end of season 5.

Coaches and hosts[edit]

The coaches of The Voice – season six

Adam Levine
Blake Shelton
Shakira
Usher

Adam Levine and Blake Shelton returned for their sixth season as coaches. In May 2013, it was announced that CeeLo Green and Christina Aguilera would return for season five, while it was also announced that Usher and Shakira would return for season six.[2] In February 2014, Green announced his exit from the show in an interview with Ellen DeGeneres.[3] Carson Daly returned as host.[4] It was reported that Christina Milian, who served as the social media correspondent in seasons two through four, was open to returning,[5] however she did not return.

The team advisors are confirmed as, Miranda Lambert (Shelton's wife) for Team Shakira,[6] The Band Perry for Team Blake,[7] Jill Scott for Team Usher,[8] and Aloe Blacc for Team Adam.[9]

With the replacement of the Knockout Round with the "Battles, Round 2", Coldplay frontman Chris Martin will serve as the sole advisor for every team.[10]

Promotions[edit]

A special look at the season was premiered on February 19, 2014, featuring Christina Grimmie's full audition covering Miley Cyrus' "Wrecking Ball". All four coaches turned for her, but her choice of coach was not shown.[15] A second special look at the season was premiered the following day, featuring Bria Kelly's full audition covering "Steamroller Blues". All four coaches also turned for her, but like Grimmie, her ultimate choice of coach was not shown.[16] A third preview featuring Clarissa Serna's cover of The Cranberries' song "Zombie" was released on February 28, 2014 to promote the second week of the Blind Auditions. Like Grimmie and Kelly, her choice was not shown.[17]

The Battles, Round 1[edit]

The Battles, Round 1 (episodes 8 to 11) will consist of two 2-hour episodes and two 1-hour episodes each on March 17, 18, 24, and 25, 2014. Season 6's advisors are Aloe Blacc for Team Adam,[9] Miranda Lambert for Team Shakira,[6] Jill Scott for Team Usher,[8] and The Band Perry for Team Blake.[7] Continuing with the format introduced in Season 3, the coaches can steal two losing artists from another coach. Contestants who win their battle or are stolen by another coach will advance to The Battles, Round 2, a new round replacing the Knockout round of previous seasons.[25]

The Battles, Round 2[edit]

In season 6, the Knockout Round will be replaced by a second round of Battles, dubbed "The Battles, Round 2", which are expected to take place during episodes 12 and 13 on March 31 and April 1 respectively. Unlike the first round of The Battles, each team will share the same advisor, Coldplay frontman Chris Martin.[26] Coaches give each Battle pairing a list of songs and each pair must agree on which song to sing.[27] Each coach is allowed one steal this round, like the Knockout round of previous seasons.[10]
