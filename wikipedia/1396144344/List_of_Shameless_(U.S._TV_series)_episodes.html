Shameless is an American comedy-drama series adapted by John Wells based upon the British series of the same name. It premiered on Showtime on January 9, 2011.[1] The second season premiered on January 8, 2012. On February 1, 2012, Shameless was renewed for a third season,[2] which premiered January 13, 2013. On January 29, 2013, Showtime renewed Shameless for a fourth season.[3]
