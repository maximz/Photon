"All of Me" is a song by American contemporary R&B recording artist John Legend from his fourth studio album Love in the Future (2013). The song was produced by Dave Tozer and John Legend and written by Toby Gad and Legend. The song is a dedication to Legend's wife Chrissy Teigen. "All of Me" impacted American mainstream urban radio as the album's third single on August 12, 2013. It has peaked at number 2 on the United States Billboard Hot 100 chart (his highest charting single there), and charted at number one in Australia, Belgium and the Netherlands.

Background and composition[edit]

The song is a tender piano power ballad, which was inspired by his then fiancée Chrissy Teigen.[1] The couple met in 2007 on the set of his video for "Stereo". He then married her in September 14, 2013.[2] The song was produced by Dave Tozer and Legend himself.[3] It is a simple, modest proposal set against delicate keys.[4]

Release and promotion[edit]

In June 2013, John Legend premiered "All of Me" during a performance on Oprah Winfrey's Oprah's Next Chapter.[5] On August 6, 2013, the full song was premiered as it was released to iTunes along with the pre-order for Love in the Future.[1][6] "All of Me" impacted American mainstream urban radio as the third single from Love in the Future on August 12, 2013.[7]

Critical reception[edit]

"All of Me" was met with generally positive reviews from music critics. Ken Capobianco of The Boston Globe named the song the album's "essential track".[8] Ryan Patrick of Exclaim! said the song, shows "Legend's current commercially friendly, sentimental sound."[9] Jon Dolan of Rolling Stone called the song a "mountainous piano crusher."[10] Julia Leconte of NOW stated that Legend's voice shines in the song.[11] Molloy Woodcraft of The Guardian also praised Legend's vocal talents on the song.[12]

Commercial performance[edit]

As of March 23, 2014, the song has sold 2,104,346 copies in the United States according to Nielsen Soundscan.[13]

Live performances[edit]

On August 20, 2013, Legend performed the song live on the Late Show With David Letterman.[4] On August 27, 2013, John Legend performed the song at The Box in New York City.[14] On September 2, 2013, Legend performed the song on Jimmy Kimmel Live! along with the album's second single "Made to Love".[15] On October 10, 2013, Legend performed the song during his appearance on The Wendy Williams Show.[16] The song was performed by Legend at the 56th Grammy Awards on January 26, 2014.[17]

Music video[edit]

The song's music video was shot in Italy just days before his wedding, featuring Legend's fiancée, model Chrissy Teigen.[18] In the video the couple "gets naked and makes love in the shower and underwater, while he serenades her with the song." The video ends with footage from their actual wedding in Lake Como, Italy.[19][20] On September 12, 2013, the first image from the music video was released.[21] On October 2, 2013, the full version of the Nabil Elderkin-directed black and white music video for "All of Me" was released.[19][22][23] The video was meant to bring the relationship full circle, as he choose the man who originally introduced them Elderkin to direct it.[18][24]

A duet version of the song with accompanying music video was filmed with violinist Lindsey Stirling and released on YouTube on October 26, 2013.[25]
