Lisa Ann (born May 9, 1972) is the stage name of an American pornographic actress, director, feature dancer, and former talent agent.[2] She is a member of the AVN, XRCO, Legends of Erotica and Urban X Halls of Fame.[3][4][5][6][7]

Career[edit]

She was born in Easton, Pennsylvania. In July 1993, she became an adult actress, but quit in 1997 due to an AIDS scare.[8] She spent several years touring as a feature dancer at strip clubs around the country, before returning to the sex industry as an agent, and later also as a performer.[8][9][10] Her first "comeback" movie was released on February 16, 2006.

Her talent agency, Clear Talent Management, formed in November 2006,[8] was later renamed Lisa Ann's Talent Management[11] and merged with Adam Glasser's Lighthouse Agency in 2007[12][13] and eventually closed in July 2010.[14]

Lisa Ann as Serra Paylin at the AVN Expo, Las Vegas, Nevada on January 8, 2010

On October 2, 2008, Lisa Ann was confirmed to star in Who's Nailin' Paylin?[15][3] parodying 2008 Republican Vice Presidential candidate Sarah Palin, as "Serra Paylin". The movie, produced by Larry Flynt's Hustler Video, portrays Lisa Ann in sex scenes with other female porn stars parodying well-known female political figures, such as Hillary Clinton (played by veteran porn star Nina Hartley) and Condoleezza Rice (played by Jada Fire). The film was released on November 4, 2008, that year's election day. On October 31, 2008 Hustler announced that Lisa Ann was going to star in Obama is Nailin' Paylin, a scene that continued the adventures of Lisa as Sarah Paylin except this time Barack Obama would be lampooned as well.[15] The scene is only available through their Hustler members website and on the Blu-ray release of the movie. It is not available on the DVD release.[16] This "bonus scene" was released on election eve November 3, 2008. On August 26, 2009, Hustler released Letterman is Nailin Palin.[17][18] She reprised her role as "Serra Paylin" in a cameo appearance in Eminem's music video "We Made You"[19] where she was cast at the director Joseph Kahn's suggestion.[13] These appearances revived her on-screen career.[20]

In December 2009, it was announced that Lisa Ann had signed on to be the spokeswoman for RealTouch, a mechanised artificial vagina produced by AEBN.[3][21] She later had her own genitalia molded for Fleshlight in 2011.[22]

In March 2010, Lisa Ann appeared in a Public Service Announcement for the Free Speech Coalition on the topic of internet piracy of adult content, directed by Michael Whiteacre.[23][24] She was selected to co-host the XRCO Awards in April 2010.[25]

Directing[edit]

Lisa Ann made her directorial debut with the film, Hung XXX,[3] released on September 22, 2009 by Justin Slayer International.[26]

In August 2013, she launched her own production company, Lisa Ann Productions, and signed a distribution deal with Jules Jordan Video.[27] Her directorial debut for the company, MILF Revolution, was released on August 5, 2013.[28] MILF Revolution won the 2014 AVN Award for "Best MILF Release".[29]

Mainstream media[edit]

Lisa Ann appeared on The Howard Stern Show as a "Snapple Girl", prior to the beginning of her adult entertainment career.[30]

Lisa Ann was ranked fifth on Complex Magazine's list of "The Top 100 Hottest Porn Stars (Right Now)" in 2011.[31] She was also placed on CNBC's yearly list "The Dirty Dozen: Porn's Most Popular Stars" in 2011,[32] 2012,[33] and 2014.[34] In April 2013, New York Post reported that she was "the most popular female porn star" according to data provided by Pornhub.[35]

Lisa Ann appeared in the 2013 documentary film Aroused, with fifteen other adult film actresses.[36] In August 2013, she starred in the music video for the song "Dead Bite" by Hollywood Undead.[37] She voiced a character known as "Prostitute #2" in the video game Grand Theft Auto V, released in September 2013.[38]

Personal life[edit]

Lisa Ann resides in New York City.[39]
