Son of Batman is an upcoming direct-to-video animated superhero film that is part of the DC Universe Animated Original Movies.[1] It is an adaptation of Grant Morrison and Andy Kubert's 2006 Batman & Son storyline.[2][3] The film is set to be released on May 6, 2014.[4][5] A significant feature will have the film as a follow up to Justice League: War as part of the new continuity established with Jason O'Mara reprising his role as Batman while David McCallum reprises his role as Alfred Pennyworth from Batman: Gotham Knight.[6]

Plot[edit]

Batman learns that he has a violent, unruly pre-teen son with Talia al Ghul named Damian Wayne who is secretly being raised by Ra's al Ghul and the League of Assassins. When Ra's apparently died after a battle with Deathstroke, Batman must work to stop his long-lost son from taking revenge and guiding him to a righteous path.
