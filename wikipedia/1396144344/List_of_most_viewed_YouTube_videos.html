List of most viewed YouTube videos

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to: navigation, search
PSY's "Gangnam Style" is the most watched video on YouTube, with over 1.9 billion views as of 10 February 2014.

This is a list of most viewed YouTube videos. It includes the 30 most viewed videos of all time as derived from YouTube charts.[1] Videos that are not included in this list include those that YouTube suspect have viewer count manipulation; there have been various cases of this.[2]
