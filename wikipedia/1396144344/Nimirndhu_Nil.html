Nimirndhu Nil (English: Stand upright) is a 2014 Tamil action-thriller film written and directed by Samuthirakani featuring Jayam Ravi and Amala Paul in the lead roles.[1] The film is being produced by KS Sreenivasan's Vaasan Visual Ventures who earlier produced films like Naan Kadavul and Boss Engira Bhaskaran.[2] The film's soundtrack and background score were composed by G. V. Prakash Kumar.[3] Ravi plays a revolutionist for a social cause.[4] The film was simultaneously made in Telugu as Janda Pai Kapiraju with Nani replacing Jayam Ravi. The film got positive reviews from audience as well as critics mainly for the sensational dialogues and Jayam Ravi's performance.[5]

Plot[edit]

Aravindan Sivasamy (Jayam Ravi) was brought up in an ashram run by a person (Nassar). The atmosphere that he grew, taught him high moral values, disciplines, dignity, devotion etc. But when he comes out of the place, his belief and learning will be questioned by the cancerous system. It unsettles him and make him fight against the immoral cultures of the society. He decides to taken on corrupt bureaucrats and his friend (Soori), a lawyer (Subbu) and journalist (Gopinath) joins him in his mission to fight against the corruption. They form a plan and successfully expose 147 government officials, including a court judge and an MP, and are caught taking bribes for Unknown person ,non-other than Aravindhan himself . Now, all those corrupted officials team up to take on Aravindan and his team with a look-alike named Narasimha Reddy (Jayam Ravi). What happens next forms the crux of the story.

Production[edit]

Casting[edit]

Jayam Ravi was signed for the lead role. He would be doing a dual role, a 24 years old young man and a 48 year old man.[6] Nayantara was the first choice for the role. Due to date issue Nayanthara turned down the offer and Amala Paul signed for the role, who would play the female lead in both the versions.[7] Meghana Raj was said to have an important role in the film,[8] but she said on Twitter that she was not acting in the film.[9] Ragini Dwivedi was roped in to play an important role.[10] R.Sarathkumar,'Neeya naana' fame anchor come actor Gopinath were also played an important role in this movie.

Filming[edit]

The first schedule has been wrapped after it was shot in the scenic locales of Chalakudy and Goa.[11] The team started a long and hectic schedule from November 20, 2012.[6] Nimirnthu Nil music, composed by G V Prakash Kumar, will be launched on December 11, 2013, that is 11-12-13.[12] The most of the portions of this movie were took place in coimbatore.

Critical Reception[edit]

Nimirndhu Nil received mixed reviews and is a commercial success.[19] BehindTheWoods gave 2.5 stars stating it as "a vigilante movie which could be given a shot"[20] Cinemalead gave 2 stars for the film, stating that it was an outdated one man mission against the corrupt system.[21] Sify wrote "The first half of the film holds your interest with some great dialogues and subtle comedy by Soori. But it peters out in the second half which becomes preachy and has three songs which acts like speed breakers".[22]
