The year 2014 will see release of numerous games, including new installments for well-received franchises, such as Assassin’s Creed, Batman: Arkham, Bayonetta, Broken Age, Dark Souls, Donkey Kong, Dragon Age, The Elder Scrolls, EverQuest, Final Fantasy, Infamous, Kinect Sports, Mario Golf, Mario Kart, Mario Party, Metal Gear, Ninja Gaiden, Persona, Professor Layton, Strider Hiryu, Super Smash Bros., Tales, The Sims, Thief, Tom Clancy's Rainbow Six, Tropico, Wolfenstein and World of Warcraft.
