Evan Peters (born January 20, 1987)[1] is an American actor. He is best known for his roles on the FX television series American Horror Story.

Early life[edit]

Peters was born in St. Louis, Missouri, the son of Julie and Phil Peters, a vice president of administration for the Charles Stewart Mott Foundation. He was raised in a Roman Catholic household in Ballwin, Missouri.Cite error: A <ref> tag is missing the closing </ref> (see the help page).

Personal life[edit]

On July 7, 2013, Emma Roberts was arrested for domestic violence against Peters in Montreal, Quebec.[2] Someone at the couple's hotel overheard a dispute coming from their room and called police, who arrived to find Peters with a bloody nose and several bite marks.[3] Roberts was released several hours after her arrest when Peters declined to press charges.[3] In a joint statement, Roberts and Peters called it "an unfortunate incident and misunderstanding," and stated that they "are working together to move past it."[2] Media reports on January 6, 2014 said they had become engaged over the holidays.[4]
