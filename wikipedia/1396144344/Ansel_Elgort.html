Ansel Elgort (born March 14,[1] 1994)[2][3] is an American actor. He played Tommy Ross in the 2013 horror film Carrie, and co-starred as Caleb Prior in the 2014 Dystopian book adaptation, Divergent. He is scheduled to play the role of Augustus Waters in the film adaptation of John Green's young adult novel, The Fault in Our Stars.[4]

Personal life[edit]

Elgort was born in New York City.[5] His parents are Arthur Elgort, a photographer who has worked for Vogue, and Grethe Barrett Holby, an opera director.[6][7] His father is of Russian Jewish descent, and his mother is of English, German, and Norwegian descent.[8][9] His Norwegian maternal grandmother, Aase-Grethe, was in the Norwegian resistance during World War II, and saved Norwegian Jewish children by moving them into neutral Sweden; because of these activities, she was imprisoned at a concentration camp.[7][10]

Elgort has two older siblings, Warren, a film editor, and Sophie, a photographer.[11] At the age of nine, his mother took him to tryout for School of American Ballet.[12] He attended Trinity School before attending Fiorello H. LaGuardia High School and the Stagedoor Manor summer camp.[5] Elgort started taking acting classes at the age of twelve. He starred in his high school's production of Guys and Dolls.[12] He appeared in the Off-Broadway play, Regrets, by Matt Charman.

Career[edit]

Elgort's acting career began through professional stage acting, in Regrets, by Matt Charman. His first on-screen role was a secondary role in the 2013 remake of Carrie.

Elgort's first highly publicized role was that of Caleb in Divergent. Caleb is the brother of the protagonist, Tris, played by Shailene Woodley. The film, based on a popular Young Adult novel by Veronica Roth, was released on March 21, 2014.

In May 2013, immediately after filming of Divergent was completed, it was announced that Elgort would be playing the role of Augustus Waters in the upcoming film adaptation of John Green's novel The Fault in Our Stars.[13] He is set to co-star opposite actress Shailene Woodley; The Fault in Our Stars follows the story of a teenage cancer patient named Hazel Grace Lancaster (played by Woodley), and her romance with Augustus Waters (Elgort's character). The film, directed by Josh Boone, is expected to be released on June 6, 2014.

Elgort has been cast in an upcoming comedy film, Men, Women & Children, directed by Jason Reitman.[14]

Music[edit]

Ansel DJ's and produces dance/house music under the alias 'Ansolo' (pronounced An-Solo).

As Ansolo he has remixed songs like Lana Del Rey's 'Born To Die', Clockwork & Felix Cartal's 'The Fire' and Martin Garrix & Jay Hardway's .

He loves music and says it's his second passion. In a livestream (February 2014), he said, he'd signed a record deal with "Staar Traxx" and is working on his original remix/songs.

His first record is called "Unite" feat. Special Features[15] and is coming out April 21st on "Staar Traxx" soundcloud account [16]

Modeling work[edit]

Ansel has been modeling since a young age, one of his first works was for Teen Vogue in 2009 when he was 15.

Since then he has been doing shoots for magazines and with his sister and dad that are professional photographers.

He did a photoshoot for a calendar called " The 12 Months of Ansel" to charity to his old school, the photos were taken by his sister, Sophie Elgort. Other works like for 7000 magazine in 2012 and 2013.. in the beginning of 2013 he worked with Cara Delevingne for Us Vogue.

He was apart of Teen Vogue's Young Hollywood Photo shoot in September 2013 and has worked with Flaunt Magazine, Seventeen, Elle Magazine, Rolling Stone etc.
