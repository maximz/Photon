The third season of Teen Wolf, an American supernatural drama created by Jeff Davis and to some extent, based on the 1985 film of the same name, premiered on June 3, 2013.[3] The series was renewed for a third season of 24 episodes on July 12, 2012.[4]

Plot[edit]

Summer premiere[edit]

Scott McCall (Tyler Posey), Allison Argent (Crystal Reed), Derek Hale (Tyler Hoechlin) and the rest of the gang find themselves up against a new enemy: the lethal Alpha pack that has come to Beacon Hills, and Jackson Whittemore (Colton Haynes) has moved to London.

Stiles Stilinski (Dylan O'Brien) discovers that someone is committing murders and using them as human sacrifices for an unknown cause. The gang discovers that the killer is actually a dark druid known as a "Darach". Deaton tells Scott that Scott is a True Alpha, a werewolf who becomes an Alpha through strength of character and good willpower without having to kill for it, which is extremely rare. Kali forces Derek to kill Boyd. It is revealed that Gerard Argent (Michael Hogan) is still alive. Gerard had blinded Deucalion when Deucalion came looking for peace.

Lydia Martin (Holland Roden) is revealed to be a banshee or "The Wailing Woman", explaining why she is able to find dead bodies and knows when someone is about to die. The Darach is Jennifer Blake, who is committing the murders to regain power. She kidnaps Sheriff Stilinski, Chris and Melissa to use as her last sacrifices. Cora is poisoned by Jennifer; to save her life, Derek loses his status as an Alpha. Deaton explains that to find their parents, Scott, Stiles, and Allison must sacrifice themselves in place of their parents and temporarily die, though this will have dark side effects.

The trio discovers that their interactions, beginning from season 1, weren't as coincidental as they thought. After they are revived, they rescue their parents. Jennifer kills Kali and gives Deucalion his sight so he can see her true form. Peter ultimately kills Jennifer, claiming he, not Scott, has always been the Alpha; Scott and Derek let Deucalion go, giving him a chance to redeem himself.

Winter premiere[edit]

Demons called the Oni are released; controlled by Kira's mother, they attack each supernatural being in Beacon Hills, including Scott, Derek, Lydia and Kira, who is revealed to be a kitsune. They are searching for the person who is possessed by a nogitsune, a dark fox spirit who revels in pain and chaos. That person turns out to be Stiles. The nogistune completely takes over Stiles' body. Possessed, he poses a danger to all of his friends and family.

Production[edit]

Season 3 will be filmed in two parts, with the first 12 filming in late 2012 and early 2013. The first 12 episodes were shot from December 3, 2012[5] and wrapped up on May 5, 2013. The other 12 episodes of Season 3 began filming on July 29, 2013, and wrapped up on December 19, 2013; part 2 will premiere January 6, 2014.[6] Unlike the previous two seasons, which were shot in Atlanta, Georgia, this season will be shot in Los Angeles, California.[7]

Colton Haynes left after being reduced to recurring status; he now stars on the CW series Arrow.[8][9] MTV also released the first page of the first episode from season three, which is set about four months after the end of season two, and Jackson's absence was addressed within the context of the beginning of the season. On October 2, 2012 an open casting call website was set up to cast identical twins to play the identical twin alpha werewolves, Ethan and Aiden.[10]

On November 28, 2012, Teen Wolf announced five new additions to the cast for season 3. Former Desperate Housewives stars Charlie and Max Carver have been cast as twin alpha werewolves Ethan and Aiden. They are described as being "dangerous charmers with brilliant minds". Australian actress Adelaide Kane has been cast as Cora, Derek Hale's younger sister, a mysterious and beautiful young woman toughened by a life on her own after surviving the Hale House fire. Felisha Terrell from Days of Our Lives has been cast as female alpha Kali,[11] while Gideon Emery has been cast as Deucalion, the leader of the Alpha pack. Described as having "an eloquent, cutting intelligence", the new werewolf will set a plan in motion to turn Derek and Scott against each other, ripping the Beacon Hills supernatural community apart at the seams.[12]Brian Patrick Wade has been cast as Ennis, an alpha werewolf who is described as a "force of pure brutality". Haley Webb has been cast as Jennifer Blake, an English teacher at Beacon Hills High School and a new love interest of Derek Hale's.[13]

Teen Wolf announced three new recurring characters for the second half of season 3. Arden Cho has been cast as Kira, an Asian-American character who has been described as a "new student and possible threat to Beacon Hills". Her character is confirmed to be a kitsune. Doug Jones has been cast as Barrow, a mass murderer, who made his appearance in episode "Galvanize". Ryan Kelley will be the newest member of the Beacon County Sheriff's Department in Teen Wolf Season 3 Part 2, he will assist Sheriff Stilinski in uncovering the mysterious supernatural occurrences that plague the murder-prone Northern California town before becoming part of the mystery himself. Additionally, Shelley Hennig was cast, with her role being kept secret until the winter season premiere.

Linden Ashby has stated that for the second part of Season 3, "This is a season of consequence. That every action has a reaction, has a consequence. And it‚Äôs not always what you think it‚Äôs gonna be.‚Äù[14]

During Season 3A production, Crystal Reed decided she "wanted to explore other avenues of film and TV" and move on from the show, and her character Allison Argent was killed off in the penultimate episode.[15]

The doctor at the internment camp was selling medicine on the black market that was badly needed by the Japanese, as they were dying of pneumonia. A riot breaks out, and the young medic is badly burned by a molotov cocktail thrown by an old Japanese woman who is a werewolf. Noshiko is shot many times by the MPs, and the young medic dies in Eichen House in pain, as the doctor sold the morphine as well. Noshiko, assumed to be dead but actually healing and unable to move, calls upon a nogitsune to posess her so that she can have her revenge, but the nogitsune posesses her lover's body instead. The nogitsune goes on a killing spree, killing all of the staff and residents of Eichen House, as well as the Japanese and MPs at the internment camp. Noshiko, fully healed and knowing that as a kitsune, she is the only one that can stop the nogitsune, gets a katana that she had hidden, and with the help of the old Japanese werewolf woman, is able to stab the nogitsune, but the sword shatters into pieces. The nogitsune leaves the body of her lover, in the form of a fly. Kira, as a Thunder Kitsune, is able to mend the katana, and her mother gives it to her to stop the nogitsune once again. Noshiko hid a glass jar containing the nogitsune in the roots of the nemeton, and Scott and his friends may have been partially responsible for the reawakening of the nogitsune with their sacrifice for their parents. Meanwhile, Derek, Chris, Allison and Sheriff Stilinski try to track down Stiles, hoping to bring him back to normal. 
46 22 "De-Void" Christian Taylor Jeff Davis March 10, 2014 (2014-03-10) 1.80[37]
Sheriff Stilinski faces a review board, but Scott's father saves his job at the last minute. Mr. McCall reveals to Sheriff Stilinski that he only came back to Beacon Hills to make amends with his son. Meanwhile, the Nogitsune stabs himself and releases a few fireflies, sending them to go after Aiden, Ethan, Isaac and Derek. Under the spell of the Nogitsune, Derek tries to get revenge over Chris, by trying to burn him; however Chris is able to free himself and knocks out Derek. Aiden, Ethan, and Isaac nearly kill each other but are saved by Kira and Allison. Scott and Lydia join forces to enter the Nogitsune's mind and manage to free Stiles from its control, however, during the confusion, Lydia is kidnapped by the Nogitsune. 
47 23 "Insatiable" Tim Andrew Jeff Davis March 17, 2014 (2014-03-17) 2.00[38]
While searching for Lydia in the woods, Aiden and Ethan are shot at by an unknown shooter with wolfsbane bullets, and are saved by Derek. Kira's mother tries to teach her the board game that Stiles was playing with the Nogitsune. Later, Kira's father discovers that the Nogitsune and Kira's mother play the game the same way, which makes her suspicious. Lydia is taken to the internment camp by the Nogitsune; the group arrive there and find Kira's mother and the Oni, who plans to kill the Nogitsune, even if it means the death of Stiles. The Nogitsune takes possession over the Oni and has them face off against Kira, Allison, and Issac, while Scott and Stiles find Lydia and are shocked when she asks why they came when she told them not to. Allison manages to kill one of the Oni and is stabbed by another while caught off guard. Allison professes her love to Scott and dies in his arms. 
48 24 "The Divine Move" Russell Mulcahy Jeff Davis March 24, 2014 (2014-03-24) 2.26 [39]
With the Nogitsune in control of the Oni, there is little time to grieve the loss of Allison, as Scott and Derek call upon their friends and allies to make a final stand. The Oni, under the control of the Nogitsune, systematically and violently take control of the hospital, Sheriff's station, high school and Alan's animal clinic. Meanwhile, Isaac and Mr. Argent realize that silver can actually poison the Oni and destroy them. Everyone attacked shows signs of the poisoning seen in others attacked by Onis. But Scott, Kira, Stiles and Lydia defeat the Nogitsune at the school by realizing the attacks are merely a powerful illusion, and they are able to escape. Meanwhile, Derek, Ethan and Aiden fight the Oni. They are just about to give up hope when Argent and Isaac show up and destroy the Oni with the four other silver arrows created by Allison. While Aiden is attempting to kill one of the Oni, he is stabbed and dies in the arms of Ethan. Later, the injured people start healing and when all seems well again in Beacon Hills, Scott tries to teach Malia how to control her abilities. Ethan tells Danny that he can't stay anymore and also discovers Danny knew about werewolves and the supernatural activities. Derek is shown talking to Stiles about a dream, but later realizes that it was real. In a series of flashbacks, it is revealed that a wolf scratch can cause someone to 'turn' if the nails go deep enough. Kate Argent, who's throat was torn out by wolf claws, is shown to be alive and a supernatural being.
