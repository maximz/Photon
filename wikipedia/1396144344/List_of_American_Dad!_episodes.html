American Dad! is an American adult animated sitcom created by Mike Barker, Matt Weitzman, and Seth MacFarlane for the Fox Broadcasting Company. Set in the fictional town of Langley Falls, Virginia, the series centers on the eccentric Smith family and their three housemates. The Smith family consists of title character Stan, Francine, Hayley, and Steve, and their three housemates are Roger, an alien whom Stan has rescued from government capture and harbors in the family's attic; Jeff Fischer, Hayley's boyfriend and eventual husband; and Klaus, the family's pet who is really an East German man trapped in a fish's body.

Since its debut on February 6, 2005, American Dad! has broadcast 163 episodes. The series' 10th season premiered on September 29, 2013, on Fox.[1][2] After the 10th season, the series will move to TBS, where it will begin season 11 in July 2014.[3][4]

Season 11 (2014)[edit]

On July 16, 2013, it was announced that American Dad!'s 10th season (2013–14) would be the show's final run on Fox. The cable network TBS has picked up American Dad! for a 15-episode 11th season slated to premiere in July 2014.[4] TBS airs reruns of American Dad! in syndication.[3] On the show's upcoming network relocation, Barker has stated, "It's going to be the same American Dad!, just in a different place."[196] Barker departed the series over creative differences once early production for season 11 commenced in November 2013.[197]
