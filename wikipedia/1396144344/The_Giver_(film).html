The Giver is an upcoming science fiction film directed by Phillip Noyce and written by Michael Mitnick. The film stars Jeff Bridges, Brenton Thwaites, Meryl Streep, Taylor Swift, and Odeya Rush. It is loosely based on the 1993 novel of same name, written by Lois Lowry.

Plot[edit]

In a perfect world where there is no conflict, racism or sickness, every member of society has a specific role, and 16-year-old Jonas is selected to be the Receiver of Memories. As Jonas uncovers the truth behind his world's past, he discovers that many years earlier his forefathers gave up humanity in order to have a stable society.

Cast[edit]

  • Jeff Bridges as The Giver[1]
  • Brenton Thwaites as Jonas[2]
  • Meryl Streep as the Chief Elder[3]
  • Odeya Rush as Fiona, Jonas's friend and love interest[3]
  • Katie Holmes as Jonas's mother[3]
  • Alexander Skarsgård as Jonas's father[3]
  • Taylor Swift as Rosemary,[3] The Giver's daughter
  • Cameron Monaghan as Asher, Jonas's best friend[3]
  • Emma Tremblay as Lily, Jonas's sister

Production[edit]

Filming began on October 7, 2013 in Cape Town, South Africa.[4] Meryl Streep had some of her scenes shot in England, where she also filmed Rob Marshall's Into the Woods, before doing additional filming two months later in Cape Town.[5][6]

Release[edit]

The first official trailer of the film was released on March 19, 2014.[7]

The Weinstein Company has set the release date for the film as August 15, 2014.[8]
