The Fosters is an American drama series on ABC Family that premiered on June 3, 2013. The series follows the lives of Lena Adams and Stef Foster, the Fosters, who are an interracial, lesbian couple living in the Mission Bay area of San Diego, raising Stef's biological son, Brandon, along with adopted twins, Jesus and Mariana.[1] Lena is a charter school vice principal and Stef is a police officer. Lena decides to take in "troubled" teenager Callie and her younger brother Jude, who have endured a series of abusive foster homes. On July 30, 2013, The Fosters was picked up for a full season[2] and an additional eleven episodes were produced,[3] with the season returning on January 13, 2014 and concluding on March 24, 2014. As of the completion of the first season, a total of 21 episodes have aired.

Season 2 (2014)[edit]

On January 13, 2014, ABC Family renewed The Fosters for a second season. Filming began in February 2014 and the season premiere will be directed by Norman Buckley.[25] The season will premiere on June 16, 2014.[26]

Webisodes[edit]

The Fosters: Girls United[edit]

During the start of the second part of the first season of The Fosters, a five-episode web series called The Fosters: Girls United was confirmed by ABC Family. It stars Maia Mitchell, Daffany Clark, Cherinda Kincherlow, Annamarie Kenoyer, Alicia Sixtos, Hayley Kiyoko, and Angela Gibbs. Girls United premiered on ABC Family's official YouTube account on February 3, 2014.[27]
