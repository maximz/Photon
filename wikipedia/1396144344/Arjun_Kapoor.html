Arjun Kapoor (born 26 June 1985[1]) is an Indian actor who appears in Hindi films. He is the son of film producer Boney Kapoor. After taking on the role of assistant producer and assistant director in a few films,[2] Kapoor made his acting debut in Habib Faisal's romantic drama Ishaqzaade for which he recived Filmfare nomination for Best debut in year 2013.[3][4]

Early life[edit]

Kapoor was born in Chembur, Mumbai to Indian film producer, Boney Kapoor and Mona Shourie Kapoor. He is the grandson of filmmaker Surinder Kapoor. He is the nephew of actors Anil Kapoor, Sanjay Kapoor and producer Sandeep Marwah, and the first-cousin of actress Sonam Kapoor and producer Rhea Kapoor. He has a sister Anshula Kapoor. Actress Sridevi is his stepmother.[5] Prior to acting, Kapoor aspired to become a director.

Career[edit]

Kapoor's first stint in the film industry was as an assistant director for Kal Ho Naa Ho in 2003. He then worked as an associate producer for No Entry (2005), which was followed by Wanted (2009). He was also an assistant director for Salaam-E-Ishq (2007)[6]

Kapoor with Parineeti Chopra at a promotional event for Ishaqzaade

The film became a commercial success and earned positive critical reception.[3][4] Kapoor's performance in the film attracted critical appreciation.

Kapoor's next film was Aurangzeb, which released on 17 May 2013. The film was directed by Atul Sabharwal and was produced by Aditya Chopra.

Upcoming releases[edit]

His upcoming releases include Dharma Productions's 2 States (opposite Alia Bhatt) which is a romantic comedy named 2 States, with Alia Bhatt as the female lead and his father Boney Kapoor's Tevar (opposite Sonakshi Sinha), which is a remake of Okkadu.[7][8][9][10]

He will also star in Homi Adajania's satirical English film Finding Fanny Fernandes opposite Deepika Padukone. [11]

Other work[edit]

On 3 September 2012, Arjun made his ramp debut by walking for Shabana Azmi’s NGO, Mijwan Welfare Society's fashion show, Mijwan Sonnets in Fabric 2012.[12]

Arjun has also hosted a segment for the Renault Star Guild Awards 2013 along with Salman Khan.
