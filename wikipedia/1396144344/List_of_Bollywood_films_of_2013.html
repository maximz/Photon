2013 marked the completion of 100 years of Bollywood.[1] It witnessed the release of multiple big-budget films in Bollywood including a number of sequels and quasi-sequels lined up. Some of the notable sequels were: Aashiqui 2, Dhoom 3, Grand Masti, Krrish 3, Murder 3, Once Upon a Time in Mumbai 2, Race 2, Saheb, Biwi Aur Gangster Returns, Shootout at Wadala, Satya 2 and Yamla Pagla Deewana 2 .[2][3]
