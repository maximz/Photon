Varun Dhawan (born 24 April 1987) is an Indian film actor working in Bollywood films. Dhawan made his debut with Karan Johar's romantic comedy Student of the Year (2012), a box office success which he recived the Filmfare nomination for Best Debut.

Early life[edit]

Varun Dhawan was born on 24 April 1987[1] in Mumbai to film director David Dhawan and Karuna Dhawan. He is the younger brother of director Rohit Dhawan, who made his directorial debut with the 2011 film, Desi Boyz. He is also the nephew of actor Anil Dhawan and is the cousin of actor Siddharth Dhawan. He has a degree in business management from Nottingham Trent University, UK. [2]

Career[edit]

Dhawan at a promotional event for Student of the Year, 2012

Prior to a career in film, Dhawan worked as an assistant director to Karan Johar on his 2010 film, My Name Is Khan (2010). Two years later, Dhawan made his acting debut with Johar's Student of the Year alongside Sidharth Malhotra and Alia Bhatt.[3] His performance and dancing skills were appreciated, with film critic Taran Adarsh from Bollywood Hungama writing, "Varun is excessively talented, supremely confident and hardly look like first-time actor. Photogenic, charming and self-assured,shine in his respective role, enact his part with gusto. Varun is a talent one can't help but marvel at. In fact, Varun is here to stay!"[4] CNN-IBN's film critic Rajeev Masand added, "It’s Varun Dhawan who stands out with a confident, charming turn, able to tackle both comical and vulnerable scenes with ease".[5] Student of the Year was a financial success, grossing 700 million (US$11 million) domestically.[6]

Dhawan's upcoming project include the comedy Main Tera Hero, a remake of the Telugu film Kandireega, that is being produced by Balaji Motion Pictures and directed by his father.[7][8] Dhawan will portray the role of a musician in the film for which he learnt singing.[9] As of March 2014, he is filming Humpty Sharma Ki Dulhania, a romantic comedy from Dharma Productions, alongside Alia Bhatt.[10] Later this year Varun Dhawan will be joining the crew of Anybody Can Dance 2 in Las Vegas accomplained by director Prabhu Deva for this upcoimng venture a sequel to ABCD: Any Body Can Dance. [11]

Other Ventures[edit]

Alongside his acting career, Varun Dhawan supported charitable organisations and performed at many stage shows. In early 2013, he performed at Filmfare awards, Screen, Stardust, and at an international event in Hong Kong with his co stars Sidharth Malhotra and Alia Bhatt.[12] He hosted Stardust Awards with Malhotra and Ayushmann Khurrana and a segment of Filmfare Awards. In January 2014, he featured in Dabboo Ratnani's calendar with other Bollywood actors and actresses.[13][14]

Varun Dhawan performed at a special event alongside Malhotra, Bhatt, Aditya Roy Kapur, Shraddha Kapoor and Huma Qureshi to raise funds for the flood-affected victims of Uttarakhand.[15]
