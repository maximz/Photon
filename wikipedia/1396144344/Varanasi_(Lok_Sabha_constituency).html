Varanasi Lok Sabha constituency (Hindi: वाराणसी लोक सभा निर्वाचन क्षेत्र) is one of the 80 Lok Sabha (parliamentary) constituencies in Uttar Pradesh state in northern India

Assembly segments[edit]

Presently, Varanasi Lok Sabha constituency comprises five Vidhan Sabha (legislative assembly) segments. These are:[1]

  1. Rohaniya
  2. Varanasi North
  3. Varanasi South
  4. Varanasi Cantonment
  5. Sevapuri
