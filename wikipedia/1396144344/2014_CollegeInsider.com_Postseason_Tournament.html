The 2014 CollegeInsider.com Postseason Tournament (CIT) will be a postseason single-elimination tournament of 32 NCAA Division I teams. The first round started March 17, 2014. The semifinals will be played on April 1, with the championship game to be played on April 3, 2014.

32 participants who belong to "mid-major" conferences and are not invited to the 2014 NCAA Tournament, the 2014 National Invitation Tournament (NIT), or the 2014 College Basketball Invitational (CBI) make up the field.

The first 3 rounds are streamed online through the Collegeinsider.com platform powered by NeuLion. Free registration is required to view the games. Both semifinals and the CIT championship game, will be televised on CBS Sports Network[1]

Format[edit]

The Sixth annual CIT will once again use the old NIT model in which matchups in future rounds will be determined by the results of the previous round.
