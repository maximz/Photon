Georgia May Ayeesha Jagger (born 12 January 1992) is an English fashion model.

Family[edit]

Jagger was born in London, and is the youngest daughter of Rolling Stones lead singer Mick Jagger and model Jerry Hall. She is the sister of Elizabeth Scarlett Jagger (born 1984), James Leroy Augustin Jagger (born 1985) and Gabriel Luke Beauregard Jagger (born 1997), and paternal half-sister of Karis Hunt Jagger (born 1970), Jade Sheena Jezebel Jagger (born 1971) and Lucas Maurice Morad-Jagger (born 1999).

Career[edit]

In 2008, Jagger signed with Independent Models, and is currently represented by their successor, TESS Management.[1][2][3] She has since modelled for Hudson Jeans, Chanel, H&M, Miu Miu, Versace and Vivienne Westwood.[4][5] In 2009, she was signed with cosmetics company Rimmel.[6]

Together with Kate Moss, Naomi Campbell and Lily Donaldson, Jagger took part in the closing ceremony of the London 2012 Olympic Games, representing British fashion.[7]

Campaigns to date are Rimmel, Chanel, Hudson, Versace, Sunglass Hut, Sisley, and Miu Miu. Georgia has also shot with Cavalli for the new fragrance, ‘JUST’. She also appeared in the H&M TV and stills campaign for 2013.

To name just a few, Georgia has worked with photographer greats such as Mario Testino, Mert & Marcus, Craig McDean, Paolo Roversi, Mario Sorrenti and Terry Richardson. Georgia has also taken her love of photography behind the camera, by studying the subject combined with Art in New York before returning to London where upon she completed a foundation course in Photography.

Georgia has also took her creative streak into the World of design, when she completed her first Denim collection under the Hudson Brand, which launched to great success. Georgia has also shot an editorial film that was directed by Michel Hazanavicius (The Artist) with French actor Jean Reno for US Harper’s Bazaar.

Furthermore, Georgia has taken to the runway in recent seasons and walked for designers such as Roberto Cavalli, Fendi, Giles, Marc Jacobs and Alexandra Wang.

She has also recently signed as the face for Mercedez-Benz 2013 and the German Jewellery brand, Thomas Sabo.
