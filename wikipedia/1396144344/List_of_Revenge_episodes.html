List of Revenge episodes

Revenge is an American television drama series that premiered on September 21, 2011 on ABC . The series was created by Mike Kelley and is inspired by the Alexandre Dumas novel The Count of Monte Cristo.[1] The series stars Madeleine Stowe and Emily VanCamp.

As of March 23, 2014[update], 60 episodes of Revenge have aired.
