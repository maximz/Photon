Black Sails is a television drama series created by Jon Steinberg and Robert Levine for Starz that debuted on January 25, 2014.[1] It is produced by Film Afrika Worldwide and Platinum Dunes. It is written as a prequel to Robert Louis Stevenson's novel Treasure Island. On July 26, 2013, Starz renewed the show for a ten-episode second season. The early renewal was based on the positive fan reaction to the show at the San Diego Comic-Con.[2]
