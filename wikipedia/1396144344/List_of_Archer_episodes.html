Archer is a half-hour animated comedy series created by Adam Reed for the FX network. The first four seasons are set at the International Secret Intelligence Service (ISIS) and surround suave master spy Sterling Archer as he deals with global espionage; a domineering, late middle-aged mother/boss, Malory Archer; his ex-girlfriend, Agent Lana Kane; ISIS accountant Cyril Figgis; and a less-than-masculine code name—"Duchess".[1] The fifth season revolves around the former ISIS members, now living at the Tuntmore mansion, in their attempts to sell a literal tonne of cocaine. As of March 24, 2014[update], 58 episodes have aired.

Series overview[edit]

A sneak peek of the series aired on September 17, 2009.[2] The first season premiered on January 14, 2010.[3] On February 22, 2010, FX announced that it had ordered a 13-episode second season of Archer, which premiered on January 27, 2011.[4] On March 29, 2011, FX renewed Archer for a 16-episode third season that was eventually reduced to 13 episodes.[5][6] Archer was renewed again in February 2012 for a fourth season, and again in February 2013 for a fifth season.[7][8] In March 2014, FX renewed Archer for a sixth and seventh season, each consisting of 13 episodes.[9]
